Key Features

Everything You Need for
Secure ID Management

A complete digital identity solution built specifically for Federal Polytechnic Nasarawa.

Beautiful ID Card Generation
Auto-generate professionally designed student ID cards with photo, department details, and embedded QR code. Download as PDF or PNG and print anywhere.
Encrypted QR Codes
Every student gets a unique AES-encrypted QR code. The QR contains a secure verification link — impossible to forge or duplicate.
Scan with Any Phone
No special scanner needed. Any phone camera, Google Lens, or QR app instantly opens the student's verified profile. Works on printed cards and phone screens.
Online & Offline Scanning
Scan via the web portal scanner or scan the physical printed card with any QR app — both show the student's full profile and verification status instantly.
Role-Based Security
Three separate roles — Admin, Staff, and Student — each with strictly defined access. Brute-force protection, account lockout, and secure session management.
Comprehensive Reports
Admin can generate and print detailed PDF reports — student lists, scan logs, individual profiles, and statistics — all with the school's official branding.
Full Audit Logs
Every scan is automatically logged with timestamp, purpose, location, and scanner identity. Full accountability and traceability for every verification event.
PDF & PNG Download
Students can download their ID card in both PDF format (for printing) and PNG image format (for digital use). Admin can also download cards for any student.
QR Regeneration
If a card is lost or compromised, admin can instantly regenerate a new QR code — the old one is immediately invalidated, ensuring continued security.
Process Flow

How It Works

From registration to verification — the entire process in 5 simple steps.

1
Admin Registers Student
Admin fills student details and uploads a passport photo in the secure portal.
2
QR Code Generated
System creates a unique encrypted QR code linked to the student's profile.
3
Student Downloads ID Card
Student logs in and downloads their ID card as PDF or PNG, then prints it.
4
Staff or Anyone Scans QR
Any phone camera scans the QR code on the printed card — no special app needed.
5
Identity Verified Instantly
Student's full profile, photo, and verification status appear on the screen in seconds.
System Roles

Three Roles, One System

Every user has exactly the access they need — nothing more, nothing less.

Administrator
Full control over the entire system. Manages students, staff, and generates all reports.
  • Register & manage students
  • Generate & regenerate QR codes
  • Download ID cards for any student
  • Manage staff accounts
  • View all scan logs
  • Generate & print all reports
  • Suspend / reactivate accounts
Staff / Examiner
Scan and verify student ID cards at exams, events, or gate entry. Read-only verification access.
  • Open web-based QR scanner
  • Scan & verify student identity
  • View student profile on scan
  • View personal scan history
  • Change own password
Student
Access personal profile and download ID card in PDF and PNG formats for printing.
  • View personal profile
  • Preview ID card on screen
  • Download ID card as PDF
  • Download ID card as PNG
  • View scan history
  • Change own password
